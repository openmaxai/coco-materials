# Food & Beverage / Retail

AI use cases for restaurant chains, retail brands, and store operations.

## 1. Supply Chain Query & Reconciliation in WeCom

> Supply chain queries and reconciliation handled directly in WeCom; photo damage reporting replaces paper forms; workload reduced 70-75%.

::: details Pain Point & How OpenMax Solves It

**The Pain: Supply Chain Queries and Reconciliation Depend on Manual Effort, and Store Damage Reporting Is Cumbersome**

Supply chain management for restaurant chains spans multiple locations and suppliers — ordering, receiving, waste tracking, and reconciliation. Traditional methods rely on phone calls, Excel, and paper forms. Store managers spend significant daily time coordinating with purchasing. Month-end reconciliation exhausts both store and headquarters staff. Loss reporting (ingredient spoilage, packaging damage) requires paper approvals that are slow to process, hard to trace, and always lag behind actual purchasing decisions.

**How OpenMax Solves It**

1. **Self-service Supply Chain Queries in WeCom**: Store managers query current inventory, outstanding orders, and supplier delivery status directly in WeCom — no need to log into ERP or call purchasing.
2. **Photo Damage Reporting Digital Flow**: Store staff photograph and upload damaged items. OpenMax auto-identifies the product, estimates the quantity lost, generates a standardized damage report, and submits it for approval — completely replacing paper forms.
3. **Automated Reconciliation with Discrepancy Alerts**: OpenMax automatically cross-references receiving records against supplier delivery manifests, generating a discrepancy report and immediately notifying responsible parties when variances are found. Month-end reconciliation efficiency is dramatically improved.

:::

::: details Results & Who Benefits

**Measurable Results**

- **Supply chain-related workload**: Reduced 70-75%
- **Month-end reconciliation time**: From 3-5 business days → completed same day
- **Loss reporting data timeliness**: From 1-2 week lag → real-time

**Who Benefits**

- **Store Managers**: Supply chain communication time dramatically reduced, focused on store operations
- **Purchasing Team**: Real-time visibility into each store's inventory and loss data, procurement decisions more precise
- **Finance Team**: Reconciliation efficiency improves, financial data accuracy better

:::

## 2. Multi-platform Voucher Reconciliation Automation

> Meituan / Douyin / Dianping multi-platform voucher reconciliation from 4 hours to 10-15 minutes; error rate near zero.

::: details Pain Point & How OpenMax Solves It

**The Pain: Multi-platform Group-buy Voucher Redemption Data Is Fragmented, Manual Reconciliation Is Time-consuming and Error-prone**

Restaurant chains selling group-buy vouchers on Meituan, Douyin, and Dianping simultaneously have daily redemption data scattered across each platform's backend. Month-end reconciliation requires individually downloading reports from each platform, manually merging, and comparing against actual payment records — a process taking approximately 4 hours per session. Inconsistent data formats and field definitions between platforms cause high error rates, and redemption anomalies (duplicate scans, expired vouchers incorrectly redeemed) are only discovered after the fact.

**How OpenMax Solves It**

1. **Multi-platform Data Auto-aggregation**: OpenMax pulls voucher redemption data from each platform daily, standardizes field formats, and generates a consolidated multi-platform report — no need to log into each platform backend individually.
2. **Intelligent Reconciliation and Discrepancy Detection**: OpenMax automatically cross-references redemption records against actual payments, detecting duplicate scans, incorrect expired voucher redemptions, and amount anomalies — generating a discrepancy checklist.
3. **Real-time Anomaly Alerts**: When redemption anomalies are detected, immediate alerts are pushed to the store manager and finance, enabling first-response action to prevent loss escalation.

:::

::: details Results & Who Benefits

**Measurable Results**

- **Daily reconciliation time**: 4 hours → 10-15 minutes (approximately 94% reduction)
- **Reconciliation error rate**: From manual approximately 3-5% → near zero
- **Redemption anomaly detection speed**: From month-end discovery → real-time alerts

**Who Benefits**

- **Finance Team**: Month-end reconciliation pressure dramatically reduced
- **Store Operations**: Redemption anomalies identified in real time, losses minimized
- **Chain Headquarters**: Multi-store redemption data fully visible, management more precise

:::

## 3. AI Supervisor in Store WeCom & Operations Manual Q&A

> AI supervisor resident in store WeCom groups; operations manual always accessible; multi-modal planogram review deployed; standardization dramatically improved.

::: details Pain Point & How OpenMax Solves It

**The Pain: Chain Store Operational Standards Are Hard to Enforce Uniformly, Supervisor Coverage Is Limited, and Manuals Go Unread**

Retail chains with dozens to hundreds of locations find it hard to get headquarters operations manuals, display standards, and service protocols genuinely followed in practice. Supervisors can't visit frequently enough; store staff find manuals too cumbersome to reference and prefer to improvise rather than look things up; new policy rollouts are inconsistently executed across regions, resulting in fragmented brand standards.

**How OpenMax Solves It**

1. **AI Supervisor Resident in Store WeCom Groups**: OpenMax acts as an AI supervisor assistant permanently in each store's WeCom group. Staff with any operational process question ask directly in the group and receive an instant standard answer with the relevant manual section cited — ensuring consistent answers.
2. **Operations Manual Intelligent Q&A**: OpenMax fully indexes all operations manuals, SOPs, and product knowledge. Staff ask in plain language and receive precise answers. When new policies are published, the knowledge base updates automatically.
3. **Multi-modal Planogram Review**: Store staff photograph their displays and upload them. OpenMax compares against the standard planogram and automatically flags non-compliance (positioning, orientation, cleanliness) — generating a planogram review report pushed to supervisors.

:::

::: details Results & Who Benefits

**Measurable Results**

- **Store operational compliance consistency**: Measurably improved, audit pass rate up approximately 30%
- **Staff operational question response time**: From waiting for supervisor reply (hours) → instant
- **Planogram review frequency**: Supervisor in-person monthly → daily self-check by store

**Who Benefits**

- **Store Staff**: Operational questions answered instantly, confidence and execution improved
- **Supervisor Team**: Freed from on-site Q&A, focused on high-value store diagnostics
- **Headquarters Operations**: Standardization level improves, brand consistency better

:::
