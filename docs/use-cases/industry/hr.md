# Human Resources

AI use cases for recruitment, employee management, and HR operations.

## 1. Batch Resume Parsing & WeCom Intent Confirmation

> Batch resume parsing with automatic candidate intent confirmation via WeCom; screening efficiency up 5x; interview show-up rate up 40-45%.

::: details Pain Point & How OpenMax Solves It

**The Pain: Resume Screening Is Enormously Time-consuming, Phone Confirmation Reach Rates Are Low, and No-show Rates Are High**

During peak hiring seasons, HR teams face hundreds to thousands of resumes daily. Manual screening one by one is brutally slow, and unqualified applications waste disproportionate time. Phone confirmation of interview intent often has connection rates below 50%, and even confirmed candidates have no-show rates of 30-40%, severely impacting hiring efficiency and the speed at which roles get filled.

**How OpenMax Solves It**

1. **Batch Resume Smart Parsing and Scoring**: OpenMax automatically parses uploaded resumes (PDF, Word, image format), extracts education background, work experience, and skill keywords, scores them against the job description, and outputs a prioritized interview candidate list.
2. **WeCom Automated Intent Confirmation**: OpenMax sends WeCom messages to candidates to confirm interview intent, available time slots, and salary expectations — replacing phone calls with a more convenient text-based interaction that sees significantly higher response rates.
3. **Interview Reminders and Tracking**: Automatic reminders are sent 24 hours and 2 hours before the interview. Unconfirmed candidates are flagged for manual follow-up, dramatically reducing no-show rates.

:::

::: details Results & Who Benefits

**Measurable Results**

- **Resume screening efficiency**: Up approximately 5x
- **Candidate intent confirmation time**: From phone average 15 minutes per person → automated, near-zero labor
- **Interview show-up rate**: Up 40-45%

**Who Benefits**

- **HR Recruiters**: Freed from mechanical screening, focused on candidate experience and in-depth evaluation
- **Business Teams**: Role needs fulfilled faster, hiring efficiency improved
- **Candidates**: More convenient communication, interview experience smoother

:::

## 2. One AI Layer: HR Recruiting + Finance Invoice Processing

> Single AI layer covering HR resume parsing and interview scheduling plus finance invoice OCR; month-end overtime eliminated.

::: details Pain Point & How OpenMax Solves It

**The Pain: HR and Finance Repetitive Work Operate in Silos, with Both Departments in Overtime During Month-end**

Mid-size companies see their HR teams (resume screening, interview scheduling, onboarding document collection) and finance teams (invoice collection, reimbursement review, bill reconciliation) each bearing heavy repetitive administrative workloads — with month-end peaks coinciding. Both departments work overtime as a matter of course. This work is highly standardized with clear rules, yet it consumes the time of professionally trained HR and finance staff whose expertise is wasted on administration.

**How OpenMax Solves It**

1. **HR-side Automation**: OpenMax handles batch resume parsing, candidate intent communication, interview time coordination, and onboarding document collection as standard HR admin work — freeing HR specialists to focus on candidate evaluation and employer branding.
2. **Finance-side Invoice OCR and Review**: OpenMax automatically recognizes employee-uploaded invoices, verifies compliance, detects duplicate submissions, and generates review outcomes — with finance handling only exceptions and final approval.
3. **Cross-department Unified Workspace**: HR and finance automation requests are managed on a single platform, allowing admin support teams unified monitoring and avoiding resource silos. Month-end overtime is eliminated at the root.

:::

::: details Results & Who Benefits

**Measurable Results**

- **HR admin labor**: Monthly reduction of approximately 60%
- **Finance reimbursement processing time**: Month-end peak volume unchanged, human time reduced approximately 70%
- **Month-end overtime**: Essentially eliminated

**Who Benefits**

- **HR Team**: Freed from administrative tasks, focused on core talent strategy
- **Finance Team**: Month-end pressure dramatically reduced, error rates lower
- **Company Management**: Two key support departments improve efficiency simultaneously, management costs reduced

:::
