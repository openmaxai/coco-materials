# Legal / Compliance

AI use cases for legal services, contract management, and compliance operations.

## 1. Multilingual Contract Review & Real-time Regulatory Q&A

> Multilingual contract review from days to minutes; real-time regulatory Q&A available anytime; legal teams shifted to high-value strategic work.

::: details Pain Point & How OpenMax Solves It

**The Pain: Contract Review Is Time-intensive, Multilingual Contracts Strain Team Capacity, and Regulatory Questions Can't Get Fast Answers**

Corporate legal teams face dual pressure: contract review is highly repetitive yet cannot afford errors — a standard commercial contract initial review takes senior lawyers hours to days. At the same time, companies operating across multiple countries work with contracts in English, Chinese, German, French, and other languages, and legal teams' language coverage is limited, making multilingual contract review a bottleneck. Business teams encounter regulatory compliance questions during negotiations but must wait for legal teams to schedule time for answers, slowing commercial momentum.

**How OpenMax Solves It**

1. **Multilingual Contract Intelligent Review**: OpenMax supports uploading contracts in any language, automatically identifying key clauses (liability limitations, penalty provisions, dispute resolution, IP ownership), flagging potential risk areas, and generating a structured review report — compressing the process from days to minutes.
2. **Real-time Regulatory Q&A**: OpenMax's built-in legal knowledge base covers laws and regulations across major business jurisdictions, allowing business teams and legal staff to ask questions at any time ("what does Chinese contract law say about maximum liquidated damages?") and receive immediate accurate answers with statutory citations.
3. **Compliance Risk Early Warning**: For identified high-risk clauses, OpenMax automatically assigns a risk severity level and provides modification recommendations, enabling legal teams to prioritize high-risk items and improve review focus.

:::

::: details Results & Who Benefits

**Measurable Results**

- **Contract initial review time**: Days → minutes (over 90% reduction)
- **Regulatory question response time**: From waiting for legal scheduling (hours to days) → instant
- **Legal team high-value work proportion**: Shifts from routine review → complex legal strategy and major contract negotiation

**Who Benefits**

- **Legal Team**: Freed from repetitive contract initial review, focused on complex legal strategy
- **Business Teams**: Regulatory questions answered instantly, commercial negotiation efficiency improved
- **CFO / CEO**: Legal risks made visible, major contract risks identified earlier

:::
