## Quick Tips

- **Group chats**: Use `@YourBot` to trigger your AI employee in a group
- **DM mode**: Send messages directly to your bot without @mention
- **Multiple channels**: You can connect the same AI employee to multiple channels simultaneously
- **Context**: Your AI employee maintains conversation context within each channel session
